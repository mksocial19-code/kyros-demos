About Trillium

One Crew.
One Standard.

Since 2008, Trillium Construction Company has been the DFW metroplex's go-to for turnkey commercial concrete. We don't subcontract our core work — every pier, every pour, every finish is executed by our own trained crews.

That self-perform model means tighter schedules, cleaner sites, and zero finger-pointing. When general contractors and project owners need concrete they can count on, they call Trillium.

Based in Garland, Texas, we handle projects ranging from $500K to $2.5M across DFW — retail, industrial, municipal, and multi-family.
