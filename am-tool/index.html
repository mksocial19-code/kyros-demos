What We Do

End-to-End Machining Capabilities

From prototype to production, we deliver precision parts with tight tolerances and fast turnaround.

CNC Milling

High-precision 3-axis and multi-axis milling for complex geometries in aluminum, steel, titanium, and exotic alloys.

CNC Turning

Precision lathe work for shafts, bushings, and cylindrical components with tolerances down to tenths.

5-Axis Machining

Simultaneous 5-axis capability for aerospace-grade parts with complex contours and undercuts in a single setup.

Precision Grinding

Surface and cylindrical grinding to achieve mirror finishes and sub-micron tolerances on hardened materials.

CMM Inspection

Coordinate measuring machine inspection with full dimensional reporting and first article inspection (FAI) documentation.

Custom Fabrication

One-off prototypes to short-run production. We work from your prints or collaborate on design-for-manufacturability.

About A&M Tool

A Legacy of Precision & Integrity

Founded in Mills River, North Carolina, A&M Tool Inc. is a family-owned precision CNC machining shop built on shared values of work ethic, integrity, and uncompromising quality.

Over three decades, we've earned a strong reputation for exceeding expectations. Our team brings deep expertise across aerospace, defense, medical, and industrial markets, delivering parts that meet the tightest tolerances and most demanding specifications.

We invest in the latest CNC technology and inspection equipment because our clients depend on us to get it right the first time, every time.

  • Multi-Industry Expertise — Proven track record serving aerospace, defense, medical, and industrial sectors.
